Top Five Campsites

Top Five Trails Top Five Campsites Camping Tips Main Page

  • #1 Mount Sarrail

    Located in Kanaskis, No cellular reception. Relatively clean site, no showers and tents allowed. Very nice and isolated

  • #2 Mosquito Creek

    Located in Field, just 20 minutes from Lake Louise. No cell reception. Despite the name, this campsite is rather pleasant

  • #3 Johnstons Canyon Campground

    Located in Banff. Cell reception and bathrooms are available. Must book.

  • #4 Takkakaw Falls

    Located in Fields, near Jasper. Next a water fall, no cell receptions. Very nice.

  • #5 Any where in bear country

    Most likely will get eaten by a bear
